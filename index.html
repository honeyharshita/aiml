About Our Assessment Tool

Our heart disease risk assessment is based on established medical research and uses a weighted algorithm to evaluate multiple risk factors.

How It Works

Our algorithm evaluates your risk based on the following factors:

  • Age and Gender: Risk increases with age, and men generally have a higher risk than women.
  • Body Mass Index (BMI): Calculated from your height and weight.
  • Blood Pressure: High blood pressure is a major risk factor.
  • Cholesterol Levels: High cholesterol contributes to plaque buildup in arteries.
  • Lifestyle Factors: Smoking, physical activity, diet, and stress levels.
  • Medical History: Diabetes, family history of heart disease, and other conditions.

The algorithm assigns weights to each factor based on their relative importance in predicting heart disease risk.
